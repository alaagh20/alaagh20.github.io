Meat

From chicken taste to beef taste, our savory ingredients are ideal for use in seasonings and marinades for a variety of meats – from cooked beef and cured meats to chicken nuggets and burgers. We can help you improve flavor while reducing the salt.

Snacks

We give snacks flavor. In fact, from crisps and chips to savory crackers and cured meats, we help food manufacturers drive taste in new directions (while simultaneously reducing salt). For example, in oriental-style snacks - where very small quantities of our yeast extracts and process flavors produce a tastier more versatile product.
