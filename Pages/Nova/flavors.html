Creating & Supplying Flavors

Al Awtad for food supplies offers clients a wide range of natural and synthetic flavours, available as liquids, emulsions or in powder form (obtained via simple mixing or encapsulated via spray drying). Our flavors are developed according to your requirements, enabling us to offer aromatic solutions uniquely adapted to specific projects. Our products are adapted for use in a wide range of food-based applications including drinks, dairy products, confectionary, baked goods and savory items.

Further more ,our flavors can be offers in both liquid and powder form to suit the target application .

Snacks & Savory

We give snacks flavor. In fact, from crisps and chips to savory crackers and cured meats, we help food manufacturers drive taste in new directions (while simultaneously reducing salt).
