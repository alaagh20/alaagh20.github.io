THE COMPANY

About Us

From the center the Ajman Free Zone ,Global Flavors & Fragrances opened its doors after two decades of wide experience in food domain. From the outset, the company has been specialized in the creation, development, production and distribution of flavorings for the food and beverage industry.

We are working to be a global leader in taste, scent and nutrition, offering our customers a broader range of services to accelerate our growth strategy by dedicating our teams creativity and expertise to work for our clients, providing innovative and tailor-made solutions for their product requirements.

In recent years, the company has successfully strengthened its position in middle East & Africa. We are operating in Jordan, Iraq , Syria , Palestine and now we extended our operations to include Oman , Kuwait , Morocco , Mauritania, south Africa and many others countries.

Our Products

We create unique, high quality flavors for sweet, savory food ,and beverage that delight consumers all over the world.

Consumers priorities taste above all other food and beverage attributes. But taste preferences vary by region, age and more. To help brands deliver customized, memorable and enjoyable products, we can offer a wide range of natural and synthetic flavors, available as liquids, emulsions or in powder form. So, our flavors are developed according to your requirements, enabling us to offer aromatic solutions uniquely adapted to specific projects.

Inside our applications laboratory, flavors are tested in realistic conditions. Our creations are adapted for use in a wide range of food-based applications including drinks, dairy products, confectionary, baked goods and savory items.

Our Commercial and Marketing Department works in close collaboration with the R&D laboratories in order to stimulate creativity and innovation, drawing observations from consumer behavior as well as experience and knowledge gained from foreign markets.
