Registration & Payment

Registration Rules

  • All international riders & pillions should be active H.O.G.® members, full or associate.
  • Non Harley-Davidson® bike riders with Jordanian license plate bikes are welcome with the exception of speed bikes.
  • Registration is on a “First Fully Registered, First Served” basis.
  • Registration will not be full and complete until total payment of fees is cleared.
  • Registration will open on 14 March 2015.
  • Registration will close on 18 April 2015.
  • Final confirmation of registration is pending availability and approval.
  • Cancellations are not accepted; all payments are non-refundable

Registration Fees

Category Rally Package Fees
Rider in a Single Room 900 USD
2 Riders Sharing a Double Room 725 USD each
Rider with a Pillions Sharing a Double Room 725 USD each
One Night Pass for Family Members 200 USD = 142 JD per pass per person

There is a limited number of single rooms; reservations are made on a "First Fully Registered, First Served” basis.

Registration Fees include the following:

  • Hotel Accommodation at the Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort for 3 nights: 7, 8 and 9 May 2015.
  • Meals: breakfast and dinner are included; lunch will be provided for participants on the different rides only.
  • Post-dinner entertainment at the Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort for 3 nights.
  • Wireless internet within the whole area of the hotel.
  • Biker’s Kit.
  • Access to Bikers’ Games & Entertainment area.
  • Bike rides to 4 touristic destinations.

Registration Fees do not include the following:

  • Airline tickets.
  • Borders taxes.
  • Shipping of the bike.
  • Transportation to and from the hotel in Aqaba.
