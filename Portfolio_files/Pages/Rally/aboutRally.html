About The Rally

Mark your calendars… The H.O.G.® Kingdom of Jordan Rally 2015 will be taking place during the period of 7 – 10 May 2015.

Harley-Davidson® riders from across the MENA region, Arabian Gulf area, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Europe and the U.S. will be coming to Jordan for this exciting event.

This is a great opportunity for all avid H.O.G.®s to explore and discover the southern and middle terrain of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.

The Rally headquarter will be at the city of Aqaba, Jordan’s port on the Red Sea. Our venue is the Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort, www.radissonblu.com/resort-aqaba

Rides will be arranged over 2 days through the beautiful countryside of Jordan, which is for sure an open museum featuring breathtaking natural sights, lost treasures and ancient civilizations at almost every turn. To crown the rally, a grand parade of H.O.G.® riders will thunder through the streets of Aqaba showcasing their mighty Harleys.

During the H.O.G.® Kingdom of Jordan Rally 2015, riders will take part in a variety of fun activities and challenging rides. The participants will also enjoy the festivities planned to rock the night away where they can get to savor Jordanian and International cuisine, buy traditional local handicrafts or just relax and enjoy the entertainment and music on the shores of the Red Sea.
