Events / March 2015

Motorcycle Training

The H.O.G.® Kingdom of Jordan will be having a motorcycle training course to provide advice the riders need to conquer the open road.

It is designed to get our members comfortable on a bike and give them the skills they need to ride with confidence.

Sharpen your existing motorcycle skills with a range of maneuvers and techniques that'll take your riding ability to new heights.

Mar 13

Rally Registration at Harley-Davidson®

Each year a huge event takes place in the Middle East for Harley-Davidson avid enthusiasts and fans; it is the H.O.G® Kingdom of Jordan Rally that our chapter are proud to host. This year the 4th H.O.G® Kingdom of Jordan Rally will be taking place in the southern port city of Aqaba. More than 400 Harley-Davidson riders from across the region, Europe and the U.S. will be coming to Aqaba for this exciting event.

Harley-Davidson Amman & the Kingdom of Jordan H.O.G®. Chapter invites you to join us at the Rally as we launch a new era of family fun on the “Iron Horse.”

Mark Your Calendars!!! Registration will Open on Saturday 14th of March, 2015

Ladies of Harley Mother's Day Ride

The Ladies of Harley will be having a female bikers ride for the mother's day which is a great way to spend some time with that special lady in your life or just a great excuse for a ride in a group of ladies.

Mar 20

Blood Donation

Once again, the Kingdom of Jordan Chapter will be holding a blood donation at Harley-Davidson. Everyone is welcome to donate their blood. All donors and riders will receive a patch with their blood type to add on their vests and jackets.

Mar 21

Memorial Ride for our angel Johnny

Please join us for a gathering in the memory of our brother & friend Johnny, on the 27th. of March. With an aim of keeping his memory alive , this ride will be conducted to remember some unforgettable moment that Johnny and us shared as a family.

Johnny your soul and heart will forever be kept in ours.

Rest In Peace.
