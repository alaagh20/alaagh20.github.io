Welcome Note from Director

I started riding a motorcycle back in 2012. After few rides, I fell in love with my Harley-Davidson® Fatboy®. I felt welcomed by my fellow H.O.G.® members and sure enjoyed the H.O.G.®'s events and rides.

When I started my hobby, I was 50 years old and that did not stop me from enjoying the true freedom associated with riding. A bit more than a year later, I was addicted to the hobby and the brand and went for my second Harley-Davidson® motorcycle by acquiring a 110th anniversary touring motorcycle.

Together with members of the local chapter, I participated in local and International tours, rallies and events. Last year, I enjoyed a European Week Tour (Faaker See), with friends from Jordan Chapter, Lyon Chapter and Duluth Chapter. We shipped our bikes to Venice and went on a great tour that took us to exciting places, from the Alpes to the Mediterranean, that included Italy, Slovenia, Austria, Switzerland, Lichtenstein, France and Monaco.

Last Summer, I toured Palestine and the Holly Land. I visited Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, Netanya, Haifa, Akko, Naqoura, Tabriz and Jordan River. few months ago, we joined our brothers from GCC Chapters and drove to Sharm El-Shekh for the 2nd Egypt Rally, hosted by H.O.G.® Cairo Chapter. The Sharm Rally was a great riding experience. Just days ago and with 7 other chapter members, we participated in the Saudi 6th Rally in Jeddah. The Rally was well organized and drew an excellent number of participants and families.

I am honored and pleased to be the Director of H.O.G.® Kingdom of Jordan Chapter. Together with my colleagues of the 2015's committee, we hope to serve our Chapter with our best foot forward and build on the successes of the previous committees and hopefully take the chapter to new heights of success and achievements.

All in all; SCREW IT, LET'S RIDE.

Nidal Mihyar, Director
