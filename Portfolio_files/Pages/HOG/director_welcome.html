Welcome Note from Director

On behalf of the Kingdom of Jordan Chapter of the Harley Owners Group I would like to welcome you all to the Chapter’s website. I’m sure you will find your visit fun and informative.

The Kingdom of Jordan Chapter is a new H.O.G. chapter, founded in 2009 and sponsored by the Harley-Davidson dealership in Amman, Jordan.

Our most profound mission is to ride and have fun; we are also proud to be part of fund raising for various charities in Jordan.

If you are a new member I’m sure you will find out fast that we are easy to get to know and easier to get along with. I will be available to anyone who might need assistance with anything or to answer any questions you might have. I might not always have the answer but I will do my best to get them for you.

If you aren’t a member yet, come and join us as a guest and then as a member. I promise that you will enjoy the experience. Oh and by the way you'd better be ready to ride. The Chapter has extensive knowledge of the back roads of the Kingdom of Jordan and love to take you places that you have never seen before.

Wishing you all a safe and fun 2013

Samer Al-Qadi, Chapter Director & Activities Officer
